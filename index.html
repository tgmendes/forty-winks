⭐ Why this site

When my baby arrived, I read a stack of books and bookmarked dozens of articles trying to figure out sleep, feeding, and what was actually normal. Most of it was either too academic, too dogmatic, or written for a different family than mine.

So I distilled what worked into two things: a practical sleep cheat-sheet, and a short shelf of books I'd hand to any new parent. Hope it saves you a few sleepless hours.
